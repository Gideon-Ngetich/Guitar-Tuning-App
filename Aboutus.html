About Us

Tunner

AjayTuner is a user-friendly web application designed to assist guitarists in tuning their instruments with ease and accuracy. Leveraging the microphone of your device, AjayTuner detects the frequency of the string being played, providing instant feedback to ensure precise tuning. Our platform offers a seamless tuning experience, guiding users through the process of achieving optimal string tuning. Whether you're a novice or an experienced guitarist, AjayTuner simplifies the tuning process, allowing you to focus on creating beautiful music.

Founder / Chief-Executive-Officer

Amos Munyalo

Amos Munyalo, an IT student with a passion for music, is the founder of AjayTuner. As a guitarist, Amos showcases his talent and love for music. Despite his IT studies, he has dedicated himself to creating AjayTuner, a platform that brings musicians together. Through AjayTuner, Amos provides a space for musicians to share their talents, collaborate, and inspire one another. With his innovative vision, Amos Munyalo has created a community that celebrates the intersection of technology and music.
